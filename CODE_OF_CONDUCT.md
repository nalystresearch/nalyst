# Code of Conduct

Nalyst is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community. The following behaviors are expected from all contributors and users:

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, considerate, and collaborative
- Assume good intent and provide constructive feedback
- Use inclusive language and be open to different perspectives

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination of any kind
- Disruptive or aggressive communication, including trolling or insults
- Sharing others' private information without explicit permission

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers at dev@nalyst.ai. Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any appropriate action, including warnings, request for changes, or removal from the community spaces for severe or repeated violations.
